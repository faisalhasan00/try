B.com(Business Analytics)

Course overview

BCom (Business Analytics) is a three-year undergraduate course that aims to equip students with the skills and knowledge to analyze data and solve business problems using various analytical techniques and tools. The course covers topics such as data management, programming, statistics, machine learning, artificial intelligence, and business applications in different domains such as finance, marketing, human resources, and supply chain. The course also provides exposure to industry-relevant software such as R, Python, Excel, and Tableau..
