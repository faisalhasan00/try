Facilities

Highly Configured LABS

Our computer department is equipped with high configuration labs having computers with good network. Lab coordinators are always active assisting the students in their projects.

Projector ROOM

We have a spacious projector room where various presentations will be conducted. Here the students have the opportunity to explore their innovative side

Indoor Sport

In order to provide physical relaxation to our students, we offer various indoor sports like Table Tennis, Chess and Carroms within our campus

Library

The library has a rich collection of books and periodicals, including latest publications in Computer Science and other subjects. The current stock of more than 10,000 books includes both Foreign and Indian publications, Journals on Computer Education

Seminar Hall

In Seminar Hall, students get the opportunity to give presentations and seminars on the topics assigned to them by the concerned faculty. Giving seminar is a key for the students to break the stage fear and build confidence among them to bring the best in them and thereby crack the interviews and also help them in their future job opportunities. Companies also use the seminar hall to give seminars about the job requirements they have and the eligibility criteria students are supposed to meet
