Course overview

BSc Physics and Computer Science** is a three-year undergraduate course that combines the study of two fundamental and interrelated disciplines: physics and computer science. The course aims to provide students with a solid foundation in the principles and applications of both fields, as well as develop their analytical, problem-solving, and programming skills. The course also involves practical sessions, projects, and seminars to enhance thestudents' learning experience and exposure to the latest developments in both domains. The course is suitable for students who have an interest and aptitude in both physicsand computer science, and who wish to pursue a career or higher education in eitheror both fields. The course also opens up opportunities for interdisciplinary researchand innovation.
